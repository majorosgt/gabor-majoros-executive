Advisory

How organisations are helped.

Strategic advisory for boards, executive leadership, and organisations operating across complex regulatory environments — where AI governance, privacy, and digital trust intersect with business continuity and institutional credibility.

01

AI Governance and AI Compliance

Organisations introducing AI systems face a rapidly evolving regulatory landscape. Advisory covers governance model design, high-risk AI categorisation, internal accountability structures, and readiness for the EU AI Act. The goal is not compliance for its own sake — it is building governance that earns trust at board level and holds up under external scrutiny.

02

Privacy Strategy and Operating Model Design

Privacy advisory extends well beyond legal obligations. Organisations are helped to design enterprise-wide privacy operating models — defining accountability, embedding privacy into product and operational workflows, structuring cross-border data governance, and building programs that scale as the business grows. The focus is executive coherence, not procedural overhead.

03

Cross-Border Risk and Regulatory Complexity

Organisations operating across multiple jurisdictions face compounding regulatory obligations that rarely resolve neatly. Advisory covers cross-border risk mapping, jurisdictional conflict analysis, and the governance structures needed to remain coherent across Europe, the Americas, and Asia — particularly in sectors where regulatory expectations are both high and divergent.

04

Strategic Handling of Authority-Facing Matters

Interactions with data protection authorities, AI regulators, and other supervisory bodies require a disciplined blend of legal positioning, operational credibility, and executive tone. Advisory covers investigation strategy, authority engagement, escalation management, and the preparation needed before, during, and after enforcement-sensitive situations.

05

Privacy-Related Contentious and Regulatory Matters

When privacy disputes become contentious — whether with regulators, data subjects, or counterparties — the strategic framing matters as much as the legal argument. Advisory supports organisations navigating complex regulatory matters, coordinating across legal, compliance, and business teams to reach outcomes that protect both rights and institutional standing.

06

Board and Executive-Level Advisory

At board level, AI governance and privacy are no longer technical matters — they are strategic risks and reputational considerations. Advisory here focuses on translating regulatory complexity into clear executive framing, preparing boards for informed decision-making, and supporting organisations where the governance posture is itself part of the value proposition.
